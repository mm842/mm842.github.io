Research

My research interests cover a broad range of topics in the analysis of high dimensional (stochastic) systems arising in applications such as quantitative finance. My main research interests can be summarizied by following three pillars:

Dynamics of electronic markets


Modeling and understanding of trading and price formation in electronic limit order book driven financial markets

Stochastic Moving Boundary Problems


Stochastic perturbations of semi-linear two-phase systems with Stefan-type boundary interaction

Machine Learning


Machine learning vs. human-knowledge based methods and enhancemends of models by deep learning techniques
